Easy Quinoa Salad


Description

This quinoa salad is easy to make for a great summer meal. Light and citrusy, it's a whole new way to enjoy quinoa. Lime juice and cilantro give a refreshing kick, while quinoa and black beans make it hearty and filling.


Ingredients

  • Quinoa and water: Cook one cup of quinoa in two cups of water,
  • Oil: The dressing starts with extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Limes: Lime juice lends a welcome brightness. .
  • Seasonings: Season the quinoa salad with ground cumin, salt, red pepper flakes, fresh cilantro, and black pepper.
  • Cherry tomatoes and green onions: Halved cherry tomatoes and chopped green onions give the quinoa salad pops of color..

Steps

  1. Cook the quinoa.
  2. Make the dressing.
  3. Combine the quinoa with the tomatoes, black beans, and green onions.
  4. Toss the salad with the dressing, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
